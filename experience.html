Project Portfolio

FluxCat
GitHub: github.com/mschindler779/FluxCat

FluxCat is a lightweight, Kivy‑based Python application that lets you explore the transient behavior of a fixed bed catalytic reactor under poisoning conditions.
Catalyst deactivation is a major bottleneck in many petrochemical processes. Experimental data are scarce and the literature is full of simplified models that ignore intraparticle diffusion or the effect of axial dispersion. FluxCat implements a more complete, yet still tractable, description that can be used for teaching, quick‑look studies, or as a starting point for more detailed modelling.

XrdQty
GitHub: github.com/mschindler779/XrdQty

XrdQty is a lightweight Python tool that synthesizes X-ray diffraction (XRD) patterns for use in training convolutional neural networks. By generating large, labeled datasets, it enables automated quantification of mineral phases. A critical step for assessing the purity and performance of natural materials that often contain multiple co-existing phases.
